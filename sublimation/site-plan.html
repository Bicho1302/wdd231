Sublimation Station - Website Plan

This document is a living site plan and may evolve as the project advances.

Site Name

Sublimation Station

This name was chosen because it instantly conveys a central hub for sublimation printing and custom design services, making it both memorable and relevant for our target audience. Optional Domain: sublimationstation.com

Site Purpose

Sublimation Station is designed to serve as a comprehensive resource for sublimation printing. It will showcase a portfolio of our custom designs, offer in-depth guides and tips for optimal sublimation techniques, and provide an online storefront where customers can order personalized sublimation products.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A customer asks, "What are the best practices for ensuring vibrant sublimation prints and which materials do you recommend?"
  • Scenario 2: A visitor inquires, "How do I place a custom order through your site, and what is the expected turnaround time for delivery?"

Color Schema

  • Electric Teal (#00CED1): Used for headings, navigation elements, buttons, and accents. This color brings an energetic, modern look to the website.
  • Deep Charcoal (#333333): Used for body text and primary content areas to maintain high readability and a professional appearance.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Applied to all headings and subheadings, lending a modern, distinctive style to the site.
  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs, ensuring that the content is clear and easy to read.

Wireframe

The home page layout is sketched for both desktop and mobile views.

Desktop View:

  • Header: Site logo (Sublimation Station) and main navigation (Home, About, Services, Portfolio, Blog, Contact).
  • Hero Section: A large banner image with a key tagline such as "Elevate Your Design with Sublimation Art."
  • Main Content: Two-column layout with product/service highlights on the left and featured projects or tips on the right.
  • Footer: Contact information, social media links, and copyright notice.

Mobile View:

  • Header: Simplified header featuring a centered logo and a hamburger menu for easy navigation.
  • Content: A single-column stacked layout with the hero section, followed by services, portfolio, and additional content sections in vertical order.
  • Footer: Collapsed sections for contact details and social media links arranged vertically.
